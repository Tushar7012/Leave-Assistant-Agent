# Employee Leave Policy

## 1. Sick Leave (SL)
- **Entitlement**: Employees are entitled to 12 days of Sick Leave per year.
- **Accrual**: 1 day per month.
- **Carry Over**: Up to 5 days can be carried over to the next year.
- **Proof**: Medical certificate required for more than 2 consecutive days.

## 2. Casual Leave (CL)
- **Entitlement**: 12 days of Casual Leave per year.
- **Booking**: Must be applied for at least 3 days in advance.
- **Duration**: Maximum 3 consecutive days.

## 3. Remote Work (WFH)
- **Policy**: Up to 2 days of remote work per week with manager approval.
- **Core Hours**: Available from 10 AM to 4 PM.

## 4. Holidays
- Standard public holidays are observed.
- Optional holidays: 2 per year.
